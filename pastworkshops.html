私人研讨会： 11月01，数字制作培训
Private workshop: 1st of November, Digital Fabrication training

We've been having several workshops going on this month, last week we held a private workshop for a construction company from South Korea.

The seminar was held at XinFab, taught by an experienced Industrial Designer and Maker, Danny Kuo.
The purpose of this one-day course was to provide the team a solid understanding about Digital Fabrication in specific about 3D Printing (Fused deposition modeling) and Laser cutting, plus hands-on activities on how to use and set up both 3D Printing and Laser cutting machines.

XinFab offers on-demand workshops, varying from half-day workshops to long-term workshops. With the focus on educating students about creativity, technology, software, innovation and production methods like 3D Printing, laser cutting, and CNC milling.


If you are interested in organizing a private workshop for your team or company, get in touch with us! info@xinfab.com

工作坊细节
想打造自己的个性化3D打印机，但又不知从何开始？本周末你将组装自己的便携式3D打印机并学会3D打印的基础知识。

该打印机的主要设计理念是为大众提供耐用，可靠，可扩展功能的打印机，并可进行快速搭建。

10位参与者在一个周末内，与两位导师一起组装这款3D打印机（周六、周日早9点至晚7点）

在工作坊完成之后，参加者将能够完成搭建并拥有一台属于自己的3D打印机，开始自己的3D打印事业。本工作坊让你有望在两天内成为3D打印专家。

参加者将会得到1个月FabLab的使用时长，并享受会员价格使用3D打印，激光切割，CNC等设备，方便使用激光切割和3D打印技术制作打印机的拓展配件和维护件。

第一天：
- 3D打印机简单介绍
- 机器组装

第二天：
- 完善调试
- 机械系统测试
- 打印校准零件
- 实现首次打印

-3D 打印机操作技术

    - 使用Cura 软件生成G-code 基础技能

Saturday 25 July, 3d printing workshop with Sean!!

Saturday 25 July, 14:00 there will be a 3D printing workshop with xinfab staffmember Sean! So if you are interested in 3d printing sent us an email to sign up or go to our wechat account xin_fab.
The workshop will show the process of 3d printing, like using the machine, changing fillament, cleaning the bed and nozzle, slicing your model with cura or repetier and adjusting settings in Cura and how that will result in your 3d print.

Please bring your computer and download Cura before you come to the workshop! Workshop fee is 250 rmb for non-members and 200 rmb for xinfab/xinchejian members, which includes a small 3d print that you can make during the workshop.
